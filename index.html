What is a bath bomb?

A bath bomb is usually a spherical chunk of dry powders mixed with essential oils or other ingredients. You drop it into water, and it begins to fizzle and release all kinds of aromas. Each bath bomb can give the user all kinds of benefits from smooth skin to reduced redness.

What is a Bubble Buddie?

They are your new bath time ritual! We made sure to only use the best and freshest ingredients for our little Buddies. All bath bombs are vegetarian and organic.

How old do you have to be?

Baths are for everyone! We avoid harsh chemicals at all costs so even the most sensitive skin can enjoy our products.

Are our products nut free?

Our facility is 100% nut free, worries here!

Will it stain my tub?

Because we do add color to our bath bombs there is a chance there can be some lingering pigment in your tub. A simple bathroom cleaner can wash this away without staining.
